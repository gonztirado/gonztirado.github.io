About Me

Hi, I'm Gonzalo Tirado, a software engineer born in Malaga (Spain) passionate about technology and programming. I consider myself a curious and meticulous person always willing to learn new things.

After completing my studies at the university, I trained for the development of web and mobile applications. After my internship period, I joined the development team where I acquired new skills as a programmer in order to become a valuable member of the development team. In the companies in which I have worked we have developed both our own products and projects for third parties.

After 8 years as a mobile application developer, I decided to try video games development. I studied the Master of Video Games Creation at the University of Malaga and I have been working for a year as a Unity developer in a company creating mobile video games.

Nowadays, I keep improving my skills while developing my own projects as well as I develop different jobs for third parties as a freelance. I listen for employment or collaboration offers, do not hesitate to contact me.

PFC Sense - Temperature Monitoring

This is my final year project of my engineering studies. The objective of the project was to develop a real-time wireless thermograph monitoring solution using devices that could be found in the market.

The most convenient type of wireless thermograph was searched; and to retrieve and monitor the information a smartphone was used that acted as a sensors monitor. In addition, a solution for remote temperature monitoring of thermographs was provided over the Internet. The proposed solution didn't involved a high cost of implementation, maintenance or replacement.
